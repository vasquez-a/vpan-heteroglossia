Welcome to heteroglossia! This is an interactive piece playing around with different UI elements, clickable graphics, and placement of text, to try and capture some of the experiences I have had while learning Spanish.

I often find myself struggling to communicate how exactly it feels for me to learn Spanish, since it is a heritage language for me. I do not evenly fit into any sort of curriculum, nor would I consider myself fully bilingual. I hope this project conveys even a sliver of what the experience is like.
